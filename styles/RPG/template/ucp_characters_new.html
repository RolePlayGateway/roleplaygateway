Character Sheet

You can add any information here that you'd like, including BBCode. Some good ideas of what to include:

  • Physical description: what does your character look like?
  • Personality: what kind of person is this, what's their general demeanor?
  • Equipment & Abilities: in your character's adventures, what do they rely on to get them through trouble?
  • Historical Background: how was your character's childhood, how did they get where they are today?

You can also simply not include anything at all, and encourage other players to explore this character's personality through roleplaying with them.

Be mindful of this roleplay's rules.
