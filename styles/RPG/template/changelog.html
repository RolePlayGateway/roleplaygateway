Recent Changes to RolePlayGateway

02-12-2017

General

  • New front page, highlighting active settings in roleplays.
  • Achievements have been added to the site. Go ahead and check your profile, you should see them towards the bottom!
  • New Builder role available for all roleplay owners, for adding locations within the roleplay and management of the world.
  • The notification tracker works, and displays recent posts that tag your characters. (Warning: Clicking the link may may mark notifications as read)
  • Completed roleplays are now listed on user profiles above the "Universes Created" section.

01-30-2013

Roleplays

  • Six new roleplay-only BBCodes have been added: [character:{numericID}], [character:{Name}], [place:{numericID}], [place:{Name}], [group:{numericID}], and [group:{Name}]. Each are case-sensitive, have no closing tag, and allow for the insertion of a custom display of each entity type in any part of your roleplay. Groups, for example, can now be embedded in place pages.
  • Groups can now be created directly on your roleplay's page, without using console (chat) commands.
  • Groups can now have logos assigned to them!
  • Characters can now be added to Groups without console (chat) commands.
  • Places can now be created directly on your roleplay's page, without using console (chat) commands.
  • In addition to the directional map, Place pages now visibly show their hierarchy (what they're a part of, what is located inside of a location).
  • The full place list for roleplays is now visibly hierarchical, with a dotted line indicating every location's parent. This makes it easier for your players to understand your roleplay's universe by allowing them to get directly at the content they're looking for.
  • Game Masters added via the /addGameMaster {UserName} command will now be displayed on all of the roleplay's pages.
  • Players granted sovereignty over locations in a roleplay via /giveSov {UserName} can now create more than 10 locations, based on a sliding scale of how much roleplay has taken place in their existing locations.
  • The time and date of the most recent post in every location now appear on the "Places" tab of every roleplay.
  • A new page has been created to view all locations in a roleplay, sorted by where the most recent activity has been.

Chat

  • The time limit for backscroll has been removed, and will now show the 25 most recent posts in every location. Private messages will now fade away after 15 minutes of time, placing the focus on roleplay.
  • The list of Places in the chat now displays a link to view all posts ever made at a location.

RPG Directory

  • The rankings for roleplaying sites have been changed to a new algorithm.

Roleplays

  • Posts in roleplays can now be added to "Bundles", accessible via the button to the right of all posts.
  • The listing of all roleplays has changed order to focus more on newly created roleplays.

Chat

  • Chat statistics now update once per day.

Roleplays

2-4-2012

Roleplays

  • You can now tag multiple characters in any post in a roleplay, even old posts. When a character is tagged in a post, the post will show up on that character's page, regardless of who wrote the post itself.

Chat

Chat

10-12-2011

Chat

Roleplays

Roleplays

  • All groups now have a dedicated page available by clicking on the Group name.

10-03-2011

Roleplays

  • If you make a mistake creating a roleplay, we now save all of the fields so you don't have to fill them out twice.
  • All roleplays now have a master list of places available at /places.
  • All roleplays now have a master list of characters available at /characters.

Characters

  • Character sheets now have a full history of all posts that character has been tagged in.

9-26-2011

General

  • Rules page now super-easy to link to: roleplaygateway.com/rules
  • Any and all characters can now be abandoned.
  • Shutting down RPG News to focus on RolePlayGateway proper.
  • Topics that exist inside a roleplay's forum now automatically send users to the new location.

World Building

  • All roleplays that have used the /build command now an auto-generated map on their "Places" page.

Roleplay Management

  • An activity log can now be found on the "My Roleplays" page.
  • Fixed tab-completion in chat for usernames with various types of quotations.

  • Added a new subpage for roleplays to be able to view all characters in a roleplay.
  • Add the ability for GMs to remove players from their roleplay.
